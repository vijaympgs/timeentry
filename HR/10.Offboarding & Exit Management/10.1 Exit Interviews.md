## 10.1 Exit Interviews

## 10.1.1 Business Purpose & Scope

## 10.1.1.1 Business Purpose

Exit Interviews management provides a systematic framework for conducting structured interviews with departing employees to gather comprehensive feedback about their employment experience. This enables organizations to identify improvement opportunities, understand turnover drivers, capture institutional knowledge, and implement data-driven retention strategies while ensuring compliance with legal requirements and maintaining positive alumni relationships.

## 10.1.1.2 Business Scope

Enable comprehensive exit interview management including interview scheduling, question template management, response collection, sentiment analysis, trend identification, action item tracking, and compliance reporting. The system supports automated workflows, multi-mode interviews (face-to-face, virtual, self-service), analytics dashboards, and integration with HR analytics while ensuring data privacy, confidentiality, and alignment with organizational improvement initiatives.
