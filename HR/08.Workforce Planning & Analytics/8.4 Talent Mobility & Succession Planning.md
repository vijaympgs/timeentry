# 8.4 Talent Mobility & Succession Planning

## 8.4.1 Business Purpose

Talent Mobility & Succession Planning ensures critical roles are continuously filled with qualified internal talent, fostering career development, reducing external hiring costs, and maintaining organizational resilience.

## 8.4.2 Business Scope

Enable end‑to‑end talent mobility and succession processes, including:

- **Talent Pools** – Create and maintain internal talent pools based on skills, performance, and potential.
- **Internal Job Posting** – Provide a transparent marketplace for employees to apply to open roles.
- **Succession Identification** – Identify high‑potential employees for critical positions and map development pathways.
- **Career Pathing** – Define clear progression routes and required competencies for each role.
- **Mobility Analytics** – Track internal moves, time‑to‑fill, and impact on performance.

## 8.4.3 Key Components

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Talent Pool Registry | Centralized database of employees grouped by skill sets and readiness levels. |
| Mobility Workflow | Process for request, approval, and execution of internal transfers. |
| Succession Matrix | Mapping of critical roles to potential successors with readiness scores. |
| Development Plans | Individual learning and project assignments to close skill gaps. |
| Reporting Dashboard | Metrics on internal hires, promotion rates, and succession coverage. |

## 8.4.4 Workflow & Approvals

1. **Request Initiation** – Hiring manager submits an internal mobility request with justification.  
2. **Talent Review** – Talent acquisition reviews candidate pool and recommends suitable internal candidates.  
3. **Manager Approval** – Current manager approves candidate’s move, ensuring team continuity.  
4. **HR Confirmation** – HR updates employee records, compensation, and onboarding for the new role.  
5. **Succession Review** – For critical roles, the succession committee validates the chosen successor and development plan.

## 8.4.5 Metrics & KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|-----|------------|--------|
| **Internal Mobility Rate** | % of hires filled by internal candidates | ≥ 30 % |
| **Time‑to‑Fill (Internal)** | Average days to complete an internal transfer | ≤ 20 days |
| **Succession Coverage** | % of critical roles with at least one ready successor | ≥ 80 % |
| **Promotion Rate** | % of employees promoted within a year | ≥ 15 % |
| **Retention of High‑Potential** | Retention rate of identified successors | ≥ 90 % |

## 8.4.6 Best Practices

- **Transparent Communication** – Publish internal job postings widely and provide clear criteria.  
- **Skill Mapping** – Maintain up‑to‑date skill inventories to match talent to opportunities.  
- **Development Investment** – Offer targeted learning, stretch assignments, and mentorship for successors.  
- **Data‑Driven Decisions** – Use analytics to identify bottlenecks and optimize mobility pathways.  
- **Leadership Sponsorship** – Secure executive support for succession planning initiatives.
