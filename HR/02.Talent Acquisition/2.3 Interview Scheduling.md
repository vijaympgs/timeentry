# 2.3 Interview Scheduling

## 2.3.1 Business Purpose

Interview Scheduling provides a streamlined system for coordinating, managing, and tracking all interview activities throughout the recruitment process. It enables automated scheduling, calendar integration, interviewer availability management, and real-time coordination while ensuring optimal interviewer-candidate matching, reducing scheduling conflicts, and enhancing the overall interview experience for both candidates and interviewers.

## 2.3.2 Business Scope

Enable comprehensive interview scheduling including automated calendar integration, interviewer availability checking, conflict resolution, multi-interviewer coordination, interview room/resource booking, and candidate communication. The system supports various interview types, automated reminders, rescheduling workflows, and integration with assessment tools while maintaining interviewer preferences and optimizing scheduling efficiency.
