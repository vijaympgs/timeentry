# 6.3 Skill Assessment

## 6.3.1 Business Purpose

Skill Assessment provides comprehensive evaluation and tracking of employee competencies, skills gaps, and development needs. This ensures objective skill measurement, targeted development planning, and alignment with organizational requirements while supporting various assessment methodologies, competency frameworks, and integration with performance management and learning systems.

## 6.3.2 Business Scope

Enable complete skill assessment management including competency framework design, skill evaluation, gap analysis, and development planning. The system supports multiple assessment types, automated scoring, competency mapping, and audit trails while ensuring accuracy, consistency, and effectiveness across all skill assessment processes and organizational levels.
