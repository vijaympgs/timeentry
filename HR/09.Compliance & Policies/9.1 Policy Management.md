# 9.1 Policy Management

## Business Purpose

Policy Management provides comprehensive governance framework for creating, managing, distributing, and tracking organizational policies and procedures. This ensures regulatory compliance, standardization of processes, and effective communication of organizational requirements while maintaining audit trails, version control, and acknowledgment tracking across all policy lifecycle stages.

## Business Scope

Enable complete policy lifecycle management including creation, review, approval, distribution, acknowledgment, and archival. The system supports multiple policy types, automated workflows, compliance tracking, and audit readiness while ensuring accuracy, accessibility, and effectiveness across all organizational levels and jurisdictions.

## 9.1.1 Policy Creation & Authoring

### Policy Master Schema

```
Table: policy

- id: UUID (Primary Key)
- company_id: UUID (Foreign Key to company)
- policy_code: VARCHAR(50) (Required, Indexed)
- policy_name: VARCHAR(200) (Required)
- policy_type: ENUM('HR Policy', 'Safety Policy', 'IT Policy', 'Financial Policy', 'Operational Policy', 'Compliance Policy', 'Legal Policy', 'Ethics Policy')
- policy_category: ENUM('Mandatory', 'Advisory', 'Procedural', 'Guideline', 'Standard', 'Regulation', 'Best Practice')
- policy_description: TEXT (Required)
- policy_purpose: TEXT (Required)
- policy_scope: TEXT (Required)
- policy_objectives: JSON (Specific policy objectives and goals)
- target_audience: JSON (Target audience and applicability)
- compliance_requirements: JSON (Regulatory and compliance requirements)
- risk_assessment: JSON (Policy risk assessment and mitigation)
- approval_workflow_id: UUID (Foreign Key to approval_workflow)
- policy_status: ENUM('Draft', 'Under Review', 'Approved', 'Published', 'Effective', 'Suspended', 'Expired', 'Archived')
- priority: ENUM('Low', 'Medium', 'High', 'Critical')
- effectiveness_rating: DECIMAL(3,2) (Default: 0.00)
- compliance_rating: DECIMAL(3,2) (Default: 0.00)
- is_active: BOOLEAN (Default: TRUE)
- effective_from: DATE (Required)
- effective_to: DATE
- review_frequency: ENUM('Annually', 'Bi-Annually', 'Every 3 Years', 'As Needed', 'Never')
- next_review_date: DATE
- created_by_user_id: UUID (Foreign Key to user)
- updated_by_user_id: UUID (Foreign Key to user)
- created_at: TIMESTAMP (Auto)
- updated_at: TIMESTAMP (Auto)

Indexes:
- PRIMARY KEY (id)
- UNIQUE KEY uk_policy_company_code (company_id, policy_code)
- INDEX idx_company_id (company_id)
- INDEX idx_policy_type (policy_type)
- INDEX idx_policy_category (policy_category)
- INDEX idx_policy_status (policy_status)
- INDEX idx_priority (priority)
- INDEX idx_effectiveness_rating (effectiveness_rating)
- INDEX idx_compliance_rating (compliance_rating)
- INDEX idx_is_active (is_active)
- INDEX idx_effective_from (effective_from)

- fk_policy_company FOREIGN KEY (company_id) REFERENCES company(id)
- fk_policy_approval_workflow FOREIGN KEY (approval_workflow_id) REFERENCES approval_workflow(id)
