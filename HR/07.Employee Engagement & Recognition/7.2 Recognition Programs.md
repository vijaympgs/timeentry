# 7.2 Recognition Programs

## 7.2.1 Business Purpose

The Recognition Programs module provides a structured framework for acknowledging and rewarding employee contributions, fostering a culture of appreciation, and driving engagement and retention across the organization.

## 7.2.2 Business Scope

- **Program Design** – Create, configure, and manage multiple recognition programs (e.g., peer‑to‑peer, manager‑driven, milestone awards).
- **Eligibility & Rules** – Define eligibility criteria, award limits, and redemption policies.
- **Nomination & Approval** – Workflow for submitting nominations, multi‑level approvals, and audit trails.
- **Reward Catalog** – Manage a catalog of reward types (points, badges, vouchers, experiences) with inventory tracking.
- **Distribution & Redemption** – Automated delivery of rewards via email, portal, or third‑party providers.
- **Analytics & Reporting** – Track participation, award distribution, ROI, and impact on engagement metrics.
- **Integration** – Connect with HRIS, payroll, learning platforms, and communication tools.

## 7.2.9 Workflow & Approvals

1. **Nomination Submission** – Employee submits nomination via portal.  
2. **Manager Review** – Direct manager reviews and approves/rejects.  
3. **HR Validation** – HR verifies eligibility, budget, and compliance.  
4. **Reward Assignment** – Approved nomination is linked to a reward from the catalog.  
5. **Distribution** – Reward is dispatched through the selected channel.  
6. **Feedback Loop** – Recipient acknowledges receipt; data captured for analytics.

## 7.2.10 KPIs & Metrics

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|-----|------------|--------|
| **Nomination Volume** | Number of nominations submitted per month | ≥ 200 |
| **Approval Rate** | % of nominations approved | ≥ 85 % |
| **Reward Redemption Rate** | % of awarded rewards redeemed | ≥ 90 % |
| **Engagement Impact** | Increase in employee engagement score post‑recognition | +5 pts |
| **Cost per Recognition** | Average monetary cost per award | ≤ $50 |
| **Time to Fulfill** | Avg. time from nomination to reward delivery | ≤ 3 days |

## 7.2.11 Best Practices

- **Clear Criteria** – Publish eligibility rules and award guidelines.  
- **Timely Recognition** – Automate notifications to ensure prompt acknowledgment.  
- **Diverse Rewards** – Offer a mix of monetary and non‑monetary options.  
- **Data Privacy** – Protect nominee and nominator personal data; comply with GDPR/CCPA.  
- **Continuous Improvement** – Review program effectiveness quarterly and adjust incentives.
