# 3.4 Tax Deductions

## 3.4.1 Business Purpose

Tax Deductions management provides comprehensive calculation, withholding, and reporting of all applicable taxes including federal, state, local, and international tax obligations. This ensures regulatory compliance, accurate tax calculations, timely filings, proper employee documentation, and audit readiness while supporting complex tax scenarios, multi-jurisdiction requirements, and integration with payroll processing systems.

## 3.4.2 Business Scope

Enable complete tax deductions management including federal income tax withholding, state and local tax calculations, Social Security and Medicare deductions, unemployment insurance, disability insurance, and other statutory deductions. The system supports automated tax calculations, compliance monitoring, tax form generation, electronic filing, year-end reporting, and audit trails while ensuring accuracy, timeliness, and regulatory compliance across all tax jurisdictions.
