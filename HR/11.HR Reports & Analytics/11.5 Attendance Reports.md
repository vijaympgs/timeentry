# 11.5 Attendance Reports

## Business Purpose & Scope

Attendance Reports provide comprehensive insights into employee attendance patterns, productivity metrics, and compliance tracking. This enables organizations to monitor workforce availability, optimize scheduling, control absenteeism costs, ensure labor law compliance, improve operational efficiency, and support workforce planning while providing data-driven attendance management and strategic decision-making capabilities.

## Report Specifications

### Standard Attendance Reports
- **Daily Attendance Dashboard**: Real-time attendance status and coverage
- **Weekly Attendance Summary**: Weekly attendance patterns and trends
- **Monthly Attendance Analysis**: Comprehensive monthly attendance assessment
- **Quarterly Attendance Review**: Strategic quarterly attendance performance
- **Annual Attendance Report**: Year-end attendance analysis and trends

### Departmental Attendance Reports
- **Department-wise Attendance**: Attendance rates by department and team
- **Shift Coverage Analysis**: Shift coverage and staffing optimization
- **Manager Attendance Impact**: Manager effectiveness on team attendance
- **Attendance Hotspot Report**: High absenteeism area identification

### Leave Management Reports
- **Leave Utilization Analysis**: Leave usage patterns and balance tracking
- **Leave Cost Impact**: Financial impact of leave on operations
- **Leave Compliance Report**: Regulatory compliance and policy adherence
- **Leave Forecast Report**: Leave demand prediction and planning

### Compliance & Audit Reports
- **Attendance Compliance Dashboard**: Real-time compliance monitoring
- **Labor Law Compliance**: Regulatory adherence and risk assessment
- **Audit Trail Report**: Attendance documentation and evidence
- **Violation Tracking**: Compliance violations and resolution tracking
