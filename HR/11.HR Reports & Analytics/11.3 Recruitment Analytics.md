# 11.3 Recruitment Analytics

## Business Purpose & Scope

Recruitment Analytics provides comprehensive insights into talent acquisition effectiveness, recruitment process efficiency, and hiring quality metrics. This enables organizations to optimize recruitment strategies, reduce time-to-hire, improve candidate experience, enhance hiring manager satisfaction, control recruitment costs, and build talent pipelines while supporting data-driven recruitment decisions and workforce planning initiatives.

## Report Specifications

### Standard Recruitment Reports
- **Daily Recruitment Dashboard**: Real-time recruitment metrics and activities
- **Weekly Recruitment Summary**: Weekly hiring activities and trends
- **Monthly Recruitment Analysis**: Comprehensive monthly recruitment assessment
- **Quarterly Recruitment Review**: Strategic quarterly recruitment performance
- **Annual Recruitment Report**: Year-end recruitment analysis and trends

### Departmental Recruitment Reports
- **Department-wise Hiring Metrics**: Recruitment performance by department
- **Manager Satisfaction Analysis**: Hiring manager experience and satisfaction
- **Role-specific Time-to-Fill**: Recruitment efficiency by job category
- **Quality of Hire by Department**

### Source Channel Reports
- **Source Channel Performance**: Recruitment source effectiveness comparison
- **Referral Program Analytics**: Employee referral program success metrics
- **Agency Performance Analysis**: Third-party recruitment agency comparison
- **Digital Marketing ROI**: Online recruitment channel effectiveness

### Diversity & Inclusion Reports
- **Diversity Hiring Dashboard**: Diversity metrics and progress tracking
- **Inclusive Hiring Analytics**: Inclusive language and bias reduction impact
- **Underrepresented Group Analysis**: Target group hiring progress
- **Compliance Reporting**
