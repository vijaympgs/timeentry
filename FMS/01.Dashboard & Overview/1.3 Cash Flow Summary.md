# 1.3 Cash Flow Summary - Functional Specification

## 1.3.1 Business Purpose & Scope

### Business Purpose
The Cash Flow Summary module provides real-time visibility and analysis of cash inflows, outflows, and positioning across the organization, enabling effective cash management and liquidity planning.

### Scope
- Real-time cash position monitoring
- Cash flow analysis and forecasting
- Bank account consolidation and reconciliation
- Cash flow variance analysis
- Liquidity management and optimization

### Target Users
- CFO and Treasury Managers
- Financial Controllers
- Cash Management Teams
- Executive Management
- Bank Relationship Managers

## 1.3.2 Key Features & Functionality

### Core Cash Flow Features
- **Real-time Cash Position**: Live bank balances and cash availability
- **Cash Flow Statement**: Operating, investing, and financing cash flows
- **Bank Account Consolidation**: Multi-bank account aggregation
- **Cash Flow Forecasting**: 13-week rolling cash flow predictions
- **Variance Analysis**: Actual vs forecasted cash flow comparisons
- **Liquidity Metrics**: Cash conversion cycle and working capital analysis

### Advanced Functionality
- **What-if Analysis**: Cash flow impact simulation for business scenarios
- **Cash Pooling**: Inter-company cash optimization and concentration
- **Cash Flow Waterfall**: Visual representation of cash flow priorities
- **Currency Exposure**: Multi-currency cash flow management
- **Sweeping Automation**: Automated cash concentration and zero balancing
- **Cash Stress Testing**: Liquidity stress scenario analysis

## 1.3.3 Data Sources & Metrics

### Primary Data Sources
- Bank account statements and feeds
- Accounts Receivable aging reports
- Accounts Payable aging reports
- Loan and debt facilities
- Investment and money market accounts
- Inter-company transaction data
- Foreign exchange positions

### Cash Flow Metrics
- **Opening Balance**: Beginning cash position by period
- **Cash Inflows**: Customer receipts, financing, investment income
- **Cash Outflows**: Supplier payments, operating expenses, capital expenditures
- **Net Cash Flow**: Total inflows minus outflows
- **Closing Balance**: Ending cash position
- **Free Cash Flow**: Operating cash flow minus capital expenditures

### Liquidity Indicators
- **Current Ratio**: Current assets divided by current liabilities
- **Quick Ratio**: (Current assets - inventory) divided by current liabilities
- **Cash Ratio**: Cash and cash equivalents divided by current liabilities
- **Working Capital**: Current assets minus current liabilities
- **Cash Conversion Cycle**: Days inventory + DSO - DPO
- **Operating Cash Flow Ratio**: Operating cash flow divided by current liabilities

## 1.3.4 User Interface Requirements

### Cash Flow Dashboard Layout
- **Cash Position Summary**: Total cash by bank, currency, and entity
- **Cash Flow Statement**: Standard cash flow statement format
- **Trend Analysis**: Historical cash flow patterns and trends
- **Forecast View**: Future cash flow projections and scenarios
- **Bank Account Details**: Individual bank account balances and transactions
- **Alert Center**: Cash flow alerts and notifications

### Visualization Components
- **Cash Flow Charts**: Waterfall charts, trend lines, and bar charts
- **Heat Maps**: Cash position visualization by entity and currency
- **Gauges**: Real-time cash position indicators
- **Forecast Charts**: Predictive cash flow visualizations
- **Variance Charts**: Actual vs forecasted cash flow comparisons

### User Experience Features
- **Drill-down Capability**: From summary to transaction-level details
- **Time Period Selection**: Daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly views
- **Currency Conversion**: Multi-currency display with real-time rates
- **Export Functionality**: Excel, PDF, and CSV export options
- **Mobile Access**: Cash position monitoring on mobile devices

## 1.3.5 Access Control & Security

### Role-Based Access
- **Executive View**: Consolidated cash position across all entities
- **Treasury Access**: Detailed cash management and forecasting tools
- **Controller Access**: Cash flow analysis and reporting capabilities
- **Read-only Access**: Cash position viewing without modification rights
- **External Access**: Limited access for banking partners and auditors

### Data Security
- **Bank-level Security**: Secure integration with banking systems
- **Data Encryption**: Encrypted cash flow data transmission and storage
- **Access Logging**: Complete audit trail of cash flow data access
- **Multi-factor Authentication**: Additional security for sensitive data
- **Session Management**: Secure user session handling and timeout

### Compliance Requirements
- **Banking Regulations**: Compliance with local banking regulations
- **Anti-money Laundering**: AML compliance for cash transactions
- **Audit Requirements**: Complete audit trail for cash flow reporting
- **Data Privacy**: Protection of sensitive financial information
- **Regulatory Reporting**: Cash flow data for regulatory submissions

## 1.3.6 Performance Requirements

### Response Time Standards
- **Cash Position Load**: Real-time cash position under 2 seconds
- **Cash Flow Calculations**: Automated calculations under 5 seconds
- **Forecast Generation**: Cash flow forecasts under 10 seconds
- **Bank Data Sync**: Bank feed synchronization under 30 seconds
- **Report Generation**: Cash flow reports under 15 seconds

### Scalability Requirements
- **Bank Accounts**: Support 1000+ bank accounts across multiple entities
- **Transaction Volume**: Process millions of cash transactions efficiently
- **Concurrent Users**: Support 500+ simultaneous treasury users
- **Multi-entity**: Handle complex multi-entity cash structures
- **Global Operations**: Support global cash management operations

### Technical Performance
- **Bank Integration**: Real-time integration with multiple banking systems
- **Data Processing**: High-performance cash flow calculation engine
- **Caching Strategy**: Intelligent caching for frequently accessed data
- **Mobile Performance**: Optimized cash flow access on mobile devices
- **Offline Capability**: Limited offline functionality with data sync
