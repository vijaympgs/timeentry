# 1.4 Profit & Loss Summary - Functional Specification

## 1.4.1 Business Purpose & Scope

### Business Purpose
The Profit & Loss Summary module provides comprehensive visibility into revenue, expenses, and profitability across the organization, enabling effective financial performance analysis and decision-making.

### Scope
- Real-time P&L statement generation and analysis
- Multi-dimensional profitability analysis
- Budget vs actual variance reporting
- Departmental and product line profitability
- Trend analysis and forecasting

### Target Users
- CFO and Finance Directors
- Executive Management
- Department Heads
- Financial Controllers
- Business Analysts

## 1.4.2 Key Features & Functionality

### Core P&L Features
- **Standard P&L Statement**: GAAP/IFRS compliant profit & loss reporting
- **Multi-period Comparison**: Current period vs prior periods and budget
- **Drill-down Analysis**: From summary to transaction-level details
- **Variance Analysis**: Budget vs actual and forecast vs actual comparisons
- **Departmental P&L**: Profitability by department and cost center
- **Product Line Analysis**: Revenue and costs by product or service line

### Advanced Functionality
- **Rolling Forecasts**: Predictive P&L forecasting with scenario analysis
- **Contribution Margin Analysis**: Variable vs fixed cost analysis
- **Segment Reporting**: P&L by business segments and geographic regions
- **Currency Translation**: Multi-currency P&L with translation effects
- **What-if Analysis**: Impact analysis for business decisions
- **Automated Reporting**: Scheduled P&L reports and distribution

## 1.4.3 Data Sources & Metrics

### Primary Data Sources
- General Ledger revenue and expense accounts
- Sales and billing systems
- Cost accounting and job costing
- Inventory and COGS calculations
- Payroll and HR systems
- Fixed assets and depreciation
- Inter-company transaction data

### P&L Components
- **Revenue**: Sales revenue, service revenue, other income
- **Cost of Goods Sold**: Direct costs, inventory costs, production costs
- **Gross Profit**: Revenue minus COGS
- **Operating Expenses**: SG&A, R&D, marketing, administrative costs
- **Operating Income**: Gross profit minus operating expenses
- **Non-operating Items**: Interest expense/income, other income/expenses
- **Net Income**: Final profit after all expenses and taxes

### Profitability Metrics
- **Gross Profit Margin**: Gross profit divided by revenue
- **Operating Margin**: Operating income divided by revenue
- **Net Profit Margin**: Net income divided by revenue
- **EBITDA**: Earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, amortization
- **Return on Sales**: Net income divided by revenue
- **Contribution Margin**: Revenue minus variable costs

## 1.4.4 User Interface Requirements

### P&L Dashboard Layout
- **P&L Summary**: High-level profitability overview with key metrics
- **Detailed P&L**: Full profit & loss statement with drill-down capability
- **Variance Analysis**: Budget vs actual comparisons with explanations
- **Trend Analysis**: Historical profitability trends and patterns
- **Departmental View**: Profitability by department and cost center
- **Forecast View**: Future profitability projections and scenarios

### Visualization Components
- **P&L Charts**: Revenue, expense, and profit trend charts
- **Margin Analysis**: Gross, operating, and net profit margin trends
- **Variance Charts**: Visual representation of budget variances
- **Waterfall Charts**: P&L component contribution analysis
- **Heat Maps**: Profitability by department and product line

### User Experience Features
- **Time Period Selection**: Monthly, quarterly, yearly P&L views
- **Currency Display**: Multi-currency P&L with translation options
- **Export Functionality**: Excel, PDF, and CSV export capabilities
- **Print Optimization**: Printer-friendly P&L report layouts
- **Mobile Access**: Key profitability metrics on mobile devices

## 1.4.5 Access Control & Security

### Role-Based Access
- **Executive Access**: Consolidated P&L across all entities and departments
- **Department Head Access**: P&L for specific departments and cost centers
- **Financial Controller Access**: Detailed P&L with full drill-down capabilities
- **Read-only Access**: P&L viewing without modification rights
- **External Access**: Limited P&L access for auditors and stakeholders

### Data Security
- **P&L-level Security**: Access control at department and entity level
- **Data Masking**: Sensitive financial data protection
- **Audit Trail**: Complete logging of P&L access and modifications
- **Encryption**: Secure P&L data transmission and storage
- **Session Management**: Secure user session handling

### Compliance Requirements
- **GAAP/IFRS Compliance**: Standard accounting principle adherence
- **SOX Compliance**: Financial reporting controls and documentation
- **Audit Requirements**: Complete audit trail for P&L reporting
- **Regulatory Reporting**: P&L data for regulatory submissions
- **Internal Controls**: Segregation of duties for P&L management

## 1.4.6 Performance Requirements

### Response Time Standards
- **P&L Generation**: Standard P&L report under 5 seconds
- **Drill-down Operations**: Navigation to detailed data under 3 seconds
- **Variance Calculations**: Budget variance analysis under 5 seconds
- **Forecast Generation**: P&L forecasts under 10 seconds
- **Report Export**: P&L report export under 15 seconds

### Scalability Requirements
- **Transaction Volume**: Process millions of GL transactions efficiently
- **Concurrent Users**: Support 300+ simultaneous P&L users
- **Multi-entity**: Handle complex multi-entity P&L consolidations
- **Department Structure**: Support 1000+ departments and cost centers
- **Historical Data**: Maintain 5+ years of P&L history

### Technical Performance
- **Calculation Engine**: High-performance P&L calculation engine
- **Data Integration**: Real-time integration with source systems
- **Caching Strategy**: Intelligent caching for frequently accessed P&L data
- **Browser Performance**: Optimized for modern browsers
- **Mobile Performance**: Efficient P&L access on mobile devices
