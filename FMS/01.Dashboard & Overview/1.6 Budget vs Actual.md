# 1.6 Budget vs Actual - Functional Specification

## 1.6.1 Business Purpose & Scope

### Business Purpose
The Budget vs Actual module provides comprehensive analysis and monitoring of budget performance against actual results, enabling effective financial control, variance analysis, and informed decision-making.

### Scope
- Real-time budget vs actual variance analysis
- Multi-dimensional budget tracking and reporting
- Forecast vs actual comparison
- Budget deviation alerting and notification
- Historical budget performance analysis

### Target Users
- CFO and Finance Directors
- Budget Managers and Department Heads
- Financial Controllers
- Executive Management
- Business Analysts and Planners

## 1.6.2 Key Features & Functionality

### Core Budget Analysis Features
- **Variance Analysis**: Budget vs actual variance calculations and explanations
- **Multi-period Comparison**: Current period, YTD, and annual variance analysis
- **Drill-down Capability**: From summary variance to transaction-level details
- **Departmental Budgets**: Budget tracking by department and cost center
- **Rolling Forecasts**: Updated forecasts based on actual performance
- **Budget Amendments**: Budget revision tracking and approval workflow

### Advanced Functionality
- **What-if Analysis**: Budget scenario modeling and impact assessment
- **Variance Thresholds**: Configurable variance alerting and escalation
- **Budget Allocation**: Real-time budget allocation and utilization tracking
- **Encumbrance Tracking**: Committed expenses and budget reservations
- **Benchmarking**: Budget performance against historical and industry standards
- **Automated Reporting**: Scheduled variance reports and distribution

## 1.6.3 Data Sources & Metrics

### Primary Data Sources
- Budget management system and budget databases
- General Ledger actual transactions
- Forecast and planning systems
- Accounts Payable and Receivable data
- Payroll and HR systems
- Project and job costing systems
- Inter-company budget allocations

### Budget Variance Metrics
- **Absolute Variance**: Actual amount minus budget amount
- **Percentage Variance**: Absolute variance divided by budget amount
- **Budget Utilization**: Actual expenses divided by budgeted amount
- **Remaining Budget**: Budget amount minus actual expenses
- **Forecast Accuracy**: Forecast vs actual variance analysis
- **Budget Attainment**: Revenue actual vs budget achievement

### Analysis Dimensions
- **Time Periods**: Monthly, quarterly, yearly variance analysis
- **Departments**: Budget variance by department and cost center
- **Account Types**: Revenue, expense, capital expenditure variances
- **Projects**: Project-specific budget vs actual tracking
- **Geographic Regions**: Regional budget performance analysis
- **Business Units**: Budget variance by business unit and division

## 1.6.4 User Interface Requirements

### Budget Analysis Dashboard Layout
- **Variance Summary**: High-level budget vs actual overview
- **Detailed Variance**: Account-level variance analysis with explanations
- **Trend Analysis**: Historical budget performance trends and patterns
- **Forecast View**: Updated forecasts based on actual performance
- **Departmental View**: Budget variance by department and cost center
- **Alert Center**: Budget deviation alerts and notifications

### Visualization Components
- **Variance Charts**: Budget vs actual trend lines and bar charts
- **Heat Maps**: Variance visualization by department and account type
- **Gauge Charts**: Budget utilization indicators and thresholds
- **Waterfall Charts**: Budget variance contribution analysis
- **Benchmark Charts**: Budget performance against historical data

### User Experience Features
- **Time Period Selection**: Monthly, quarterly, yearly budget views
- **Currency Display**: Multi-currency budget analysis with translation
- **Export Functionality**: Excel, PDF, and CSV export capabilities
- **Print Optimization**: Printer-friendly variance report layouts
- **Mobile Access**: Key budget metrics and alerts on mobile devices

## 1.6.5 Access Control & Security

### Role-Based Access
- **Executive Access**: Consolidated budget vs actual across all entities
- **Budget Manager Access**: Full budget management and analysis capabilities
- **Department Head Access**: Budget variance for specific departments
- **Read-only Access**: Budget viewing without modification rights
- **External Access**: Limited budget access for auditors and stakeholders

### Data Security
- **Budget-level Security**: Access control at department and entity level
- **Data Masking**: Sensitive budget data protection
- **Audit Trail**: Complete logging of budget access and modifications
- **Encryption**: Secure budget data transmission and storage
- **Session Management**: Secure user session handling

### Compliance Requirements
- **Budget Controls**: Internal control compliance for budget management
- **Audit Requirements**: Complete audit trail for budget reporting
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Budget reporting for regulatory submissions
- **Segregation of Duties**: Budget approval and modification controls
- **Data Privacy**: Protection of sensitive budget information

## 1.6.6 Performance Requirements

### Response Time Standards
- **Variance Calculations**: Budget vs actual analysis under 5 seconds
- **Drill-down Operations**: Navigation to detailed data under 3 seconds
- **Forecast Updates**: Rolling forecast generation under 10 seconds
- **Alert Processing**: Budget deviation alerts under 2 seconds
- **Report Generation**: Variance reports under 15 seconds

### Scalability Requirements
- **Transaction Volume**: Process millions of actual transactions efficiently
- **Budget Complexity**: Handle 10,000+ budget accounts and cost centers
- **Concurrent Users**: Support 500+ simultaneous budget users
- **Multi-entity**: Handle complex multi-entity budget structures
- **Historical Data**: Maintain 5+ years of budget history

### Technical Performance
- **Calculation Engine**: High-performance variance calculation engine
- **Data Integration**: Real-time integration with source systems
- **Caching Strategy**: Intelligent caching for frequently accessed data
- **Browser Performance**: Optimized for modern browsers
- **Mobile Performance**: Efficient budget access on mobile devices
