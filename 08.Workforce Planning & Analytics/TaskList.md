# Task List for Workforce Planning & Analytics (Section 8)

This checklist follows the structure defined in **Learning01/01/00.masterindex.md** and breaks down each sub‑module into concrete steps. Use it to track progress, verify each edit, and ensure the documentation matches the level of detail shown in other sections.

## 8.1 Workforce Planning

- [ ] **Draft Overview** – Business purpose and scope.
- [ ] **Data Sources** – Identify HRIS, payroll, performance, and external labor market data.
- [ ] **Workforce Modeling** – Define headcount forecasting models, scenario planning, and skill gap analysis.
- [ ] **Approval Workflow** – Review and approve planning assumptions and budgets.
- [ ] **Metrics & KPIs** – Headcount variance, turnover rate, skill gap closure rate.
- [ ] **Best Practices** – Stakeholder alignment, data governance, regular review cycles.
- [ ] **Verify & Commit** – Confirm content and mark as completed.

## 8.2 Analytics & Reporting

- [ ] **Draft Overview** – Business purpose and scope.
- [ ] **Analytics Architecture** – Data warehouse design, ETL processes, and BI tooling.
- [ ] **Key Reports** – Workforce composition, diversity, talent mobility, and cost analysis.
- [ ] **Dashboard Development** – Real‑time visualizations for leadership.
- [ ] **Metrics & KPIs** – Report adoption rate, data latency, insight-to-action time.
- [ ] **Best Practices** – Self‑service reporting, data security, user training.
- [ ] **Verify & Commit** – Confirm content and mark as completed.

## 8.3 Forecasting & Scenario Planning

- [ ] **Draft Overview** – Business purpose and scope.
- [ ] **Forecast Models** – Time‑series, regression, and machine‑learning approaches.
- [ ] **Scenario Library** – Define “business as usual”, growth, contraction, and talent shortage scenarios.
- [ ] **Integration** – Link forecasts with budgeting and strategic planning systems.
- [ ] **Metrics & KPIs** – Forecast accuracy, scenario coverage, planning cycle time.
- [ ] **Best Practices** – Continuous model validation, stakeholder collaboration, documentation.
- [ ] **Verify & Commit** – Confirm content and mark as completed.

## 8.4 Talent Mobility & Succession Planning

- [ ] **Draft Overview** – Business purpose and scope.
- [ ] **Mobility Framework** – Internal job posting, talent pools, and career pathing.
- [ ] **Succession Models** – Critical role identification, readiness assessments, development plans.
- [ ] **Metrics & KPIs** – Internal mobility rate, succession coverage, time‑to‑fill critical roles.
- [ ] **Best Practices** – Transparent processes, talent reviews, development tracking.
- [ ] **Verify & Commit** – Confirm content and mark as completed.
