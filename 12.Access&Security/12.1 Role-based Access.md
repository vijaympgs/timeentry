# 12.1 Role-based Access

## 12.1.1 Business Purpose & Scope

## 12.1.1.1 Business Purpose

Role-based Access Control (RBAC) provides a systematic framework for managing user permissions and access rights based on organizational roles, responsibilities, and job functions. This ensures data security, compliance with regulatory requirements, operational efficiency, and proper segregation of duties while enabling granular access control, audit trails, and centralized permission management across all HR systems and applications.

## 12.1.1.2 Business Scope

Enable comprehensive role-based access management including role definition, permission assignment, user role mapping, access request workflows, and audit compliance. The system supports hierarchical role structures, dynamic permission inheritance, time-based access controls, and integration with identity management systems while ensuring data protection, regulatory compliance, and operational security across all HR functions and organizational levels.
