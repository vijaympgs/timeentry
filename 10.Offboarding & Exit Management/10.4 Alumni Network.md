## 10.4 Alumni Network

## 10.4.1 Business Purpose & Scope

## 10.4.1.1 Business Purpose

Alumni Network management provides a systematic framework for maintaining strategic relationships with former employees, enabling talent re-engagement, knowledge sharing, business development opportunities, and brand advocacy. This supports organizational growth through boomerang hires, referral programs, partnership opportunities, and maintains a valuable network of experienced professionals who understand the company culture and operations.

## 10.4.1.2 Business Scope

Enable comprehensive alumni relationship management including profile maintenance, engagement tracking, communication preferences, event management, career progression monitoring, re-hiring workflows, referral programs, and analytics. The system supports multi-channel engagement, privacy compliance, data security, and integration with HR, recruitment, and marketing systems while ensuring alignment with organizational policies and legal requirements.
