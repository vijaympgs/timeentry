## 10.5 Exit Analytics

## 10.5.1 Business Purpose & Scope

## 10.5.1.1 Business Purpose

Exit Analytics provides comprehensive analysis and insights into employee turnover patterns, exit reasons, and organizational impact. This enables data-driven decision making for retention strategies, workforce planning, policy improvements, and cost optimization through systematic analysis of offboarding data, trend identification, and predictive modeling for proactive intervention.

## 10.5.1.2 Business Scope

Enable complete exit analytics including turnover analysis, exit reason categorization, cost impact assessment, retention strategy effectiveness, predictive modeling, and reporting. The system supports multi-dimensional analysis, real-time dashboards, trend forecasting, benchmarking, and integration with HRIS and financial systems while ensuring data accuracy, compliance, and actionable insights for organizational improvement.
