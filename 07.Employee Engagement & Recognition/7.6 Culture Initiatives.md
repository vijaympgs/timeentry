# 7.6 Culture Initiatives

## 7.6.1 Business Purpose & Scope

The Culture Initiatives module provides a comprehensive framework for designing, implementing, and managing organizational culture programs that foster a positive work environment, promote company values, and enhance employee engagement. This system enables organizations to systematically develop culture-building activities, track cultural metrics, and measure the impact of culture initiatives on business outcomes.

Business Scope includes culture assessment and measurement, values alignment programs, diversity and inclusion initiatives, employee resource group management, culture transformation projects, recognition and celebration programs, communication and engagement strategies, and comprehensive analytics for culture impact measurement and continuous improvement.
