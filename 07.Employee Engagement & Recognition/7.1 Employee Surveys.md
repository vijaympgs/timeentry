# 7.1 Employee Surveys

## 7.1.1 Business Purpose

Employee surveys provide a systematic, data‑driven mechanism to capture workforce sentiment, identify improvement opportunities, and measure the effectiveness of HR initiatives. The insights support strategic decision‑making, enhance engagement, and align talent practices with organizational goals.

## 7.1.2 Business Scope

Enable end‑to‑end survey management, including:

- **Survey Design** – Create reusable templates, question banks, and branching logic.
- **Targeting & Segmentation** – Select participants by department, role, tenure, location, or custom criteria.
- **Distribution** – Deploy via email, intranet, mobile app, or chatbot with scheduling and reminder automation.
- **Response Collection** – Support multi‑language, accessibility compliance, and anonymous submissions where required.
- **Analytics & Reporting** – Real‑time dashboards, trend analysis, sentiment scoring, and correlation with performance, turnover, and other HR metrics.
- **Action Management** – Link findings to improvement initiatives, assign owners, and track remediation.

## 7.1.3 Survey Master Schema

```sql
Table: employee_survey
Fields:
- id: UUID (Primary Key)
- company_id: UUID (Foreign Key to company)
- survey_code: VARCHAR(50) (Required, Indexed)
- survey_name: VARCHAR(200) (Required)
- survey_description: TEXT
- survey_type: ENUM('Annual','Pulse','Engagement','Onboarding','Exit')
- target_audience: JSON   -- e.g., departments, roles, tenure ranges
- launch_date: DATE
- close_date: DATE
- is_active: BOOLEAN (Default: TRUE)
- created_by_user_id: UUID (Foreign Key to user)
- created_at: TIMESTAMP (Auto)
- updated_at: TIMESTAMP (Auto)

Indexes:
- PRIMARY KEY (id)
- UNIQUE KEY uk_employee_survey_company_code (company_id, survey_code)
- INDEX idx_company_id (company_id)
- INDEX idx_survey_type (survey_type)
- INDEX idx_is_active (is_active)
```

## 7.1.14 Workflow & Approvals

1. **Design Approval** – HR defines survey objectives, target audience, and question set; workflow routes to L&D lead for review.  
2. **Content Review** – Legal/compliance reviews question wording for bias and regulatory compliance.  
3. **Launch Authorization** – Senior HR manager approves launch date and distribution channels.  
4. **Data Governance** – Post‑collection, data privacy officer validates anonymization and storage compliance before analytics.

## 7.1.15 Metrics & KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Target |
|-----|------------|--------|
| **Response Rate** | % of invited participants who submit a response | ≥ 70 % |
| **Completion Rate** | % of started surveys that reach the final page | ≥ 90 % |
| **NPS** | Net Promoter Score derived from NPS‑type question | ≥ 30 |
| **Sentiment Score** | Average sentiment from open‑ended responses (‑1 to +1) | ≥ 0.2 |
| **Action Closure Rate** | % of identified actions completed within SLA | ≥ 80 % |

## 7.1.16 Best Practices

- **Clear Purpose** – Communicate why the survey is being conducted and how results will be used.  
- **Brevity** – Keep surveys under 10 minutes to improve completion.  
- **Anonymity Options** – Offer anonymous mode for sensitive topics.  
- **Multi‑Channel Distribution** – Use email, intranet, and mobile push to maximize reach.  
- **Timely Follow‑Up** – Share high‑level results within two weeks of closure and outline action plans.  
- **Continuous Improvement** – Iterate question sets based on previous response quality and metric relevance.
