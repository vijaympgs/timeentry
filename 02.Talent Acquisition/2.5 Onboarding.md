# 2.5 Onboarding

## 2.5.1 Business Purpose

Onboarding provides a structured process for integrating new hires into the organization, ensuring smooth transition from candidate to employee. It facilitates task assignment and tracking, document collection, equipment provisioning, system access setup, training coordination, and buddy program matching while ensuring compliance, engagement, and productivity acceleration for new employees.

## 2.5.2 Business Scope

Enable comprehensive onboarding management including personalized onboarding plans, task automation, document management, equipment requests, IT access provisioning, benefits enrollment, training scheduling, and progress tracking. The system supports role-based onboarding workflows, automated notifications, milestone tracking, and integration with HR systems while ensuring consistent onboarding experiences and regulatory compliance.
