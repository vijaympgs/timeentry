# 11.1 Headcount Reports

## Business Purpose & Scope

Headcount Reports provide comprehensive workforce analytics and insights for strategic HR planning and organizational decision-making. This enables organizations to monitor workforce composition, track headcount trends, analyze demographic distributions, support budget planning, ensure compliance with labor regulations, and optimize resource allocation while providing real-time visibility into workforce metrics and supporting data-driven HR strategies.

## Report Specifications

### Standard Headcount Reports
- **Daily Headcount Summary**: Real-time workforce composition and changes
- **Weekly Headcount Trends**: Weekly workforce fluctuations and hiring activities
- **Monthly Headcount Analysis**: Comprehensive monthly workforce analytics
- **Quarterly Headcount Review**: Strategic quarterly workforce assessment
- **Annual Headcount Report**: Year-end workforce composition and trends

### Departmental Headcount Reports
- **Department-wise Distribution**: Headcount allocation across departments
- **Cost Center Analysis**: Headcount by cost center and budget variance
- **Business Unit Metrics**: Workforce distribution by business units
- **Functional Area Analysis**: Headcount by functional areas and roles

### Demographic & Diversity Reports
- **Age Distribution Analysis**: Workforce age demographics and trends
- **Gender Diversity Metrics**: Gender distribution and diversity ratios
- **Ethnicity Distribution**: Ethnic composition and diversity metrics
- **Geographic Distribution**: Workforce distribution by location
- **Educational Background**: Employee education and skill levels

### Compliance & Regulatory Reports
- **Labor Law Compliance**: Headcount compliance with labor regulations
- **Diversity Compliance**: Diversity metrics and regulatory compliance
- **Immigration Status**: Work permit and visa compliance reporting
- **Equal Opportunity**: EEO and affirmative action compliance

### Financial Impact Reports
- **Headcount Cost Analysis**: Cost per employee and budget utilization
- **Compensation Analysis**: Total compensation and benefits cost analysis
- **ROI Metrics**: Headcount return on investment calculations
- **Budget Variance**: Budget vs. actual headcount cost analysis
