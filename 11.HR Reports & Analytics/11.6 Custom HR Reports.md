# 11.6 Custom HR Reports

## Business Purpose & Scope

Custom HR Reports provide flexible, user-configurable reporting capabilities that enable organizations to create tailored analytics solutions for specific business needs. This enables organizations to generate specialized insights, support unique decision-making requirements, create role-specific dashboards, develop custom KPIs, integrate diverse data sources, and deliver personalized reporting while maintaining data governance and ensuring alignment with strategic business objectives.

## Report Specifications

### Custom Report Types
- **Ad-hoc Analysis Reports**: User-defined on-demand analytical reports
- **Executive Dashboards**: C-level tailored strategic overview dashboards
- **Operational Reports**: Department-specific operational metrics and KPIs
- **Compliance Reports**: Regulatory and audit-specific custom reports
- **Predictive Analytics Reports**: Forecasting and trend analysis custom reports

### Data Source Integration
- **Multi-system Integration**: Integration across HRIS, ATS, Payroll, and other systems
- **External Data Integration**: Market data, benchmarks, and external intelligence
- **Real-time Data Feeds**: Live data streaming for real-time reporting
- **Historical Data Analysis**: Long-term trend analysis and historical reporting
- **API Integration**: Custom API connections for specialized data sources

### Visualization & Presentation
- **Custom Chart Types**: Tailored visualization options and custom charts
- **Interactive Dashboards**: User-configurable interactive dashboard elements
- **Mobile-optimized Reports**: Responsive design for mobile device access
- **Export Capabilities**: Multiple format exports (PDF, Excel, PowerPoint, etc.)
- **Presentation-ready Formats**: Executive-ready report formatting and styling
