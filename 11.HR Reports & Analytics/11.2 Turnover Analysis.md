# 11.2 Turnover Analysis

## Business Purpose & Scope

Turnover Analysis provides comprehensive insights into employee attrition patterns, retention strategies, and workforce stability metrics. This enables organizations to identify turnover trends, analyze root causes, implement retention initiatives, reduce recruitment costs, improve employee satisfaction, and maintain organizational knowledge while supporting strategic workforce planning and talent management decisions.

## Report Specifications

### Standard Turnover Reports
- **Monthly Turnover Dashboard**: Real-time turnover metrics and trends
- **Quarterly Turnover Analysis**: Comprehensive quarterly attrition analysis
- **Annual Turnover Report**: Year-end turnover assessment and trends
- **Turnover Forecast Report**: Predictive turnover analysis and planning
- **Regrettable Turnover Analysis**: Focus on preventable employee departures

### Departmental Turnover Reports
- **Department-wise Turnover Comparison**: Turnover rates across departments
- **Manager Turnover Impact**: Manager effectiveness on team retention
- **High-Risk Department Alert**: Departments with elevated turnover rates
- **Turnover Cost by Department**: Financial impact by department

### Demographic Turnover Reports
- **Age-based Turnover Analysis**: Turnover patterns by employee age groups
- **Tenure-based Turnover**: Turnover by employee tenure categories
- **Diversity Turnover Metrics**: Turnover analysis across demographic groups
- **Geographic Turnover Distribution**: Location-based turnover patterns

### Predictive Analytics Reports
- **Turnover Risk Dashboard**: Real-time flight risk identification
- **Attrition Prediction Model**: Machine learning-based turnover forecasting
- **Intervention Effectiveness**: Retention program impact analysis
- **Early Warning System**: Pre-emptive turnover indicators
