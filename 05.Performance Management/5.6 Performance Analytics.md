# 5.6 Performance Analytics

## 5.6.1 Business Purpose

Performance Analytics provides comprehensive analysis and reporting capabilities for all performance management processes, including goal achievement, review outcomes, competency development, and organizational performance trends. This ensures data-driven decision making, predictive insights, and strategic planning while supporting various analysis methodologies, visualization techniques, and integration with business intelligence systems.

## 5.6.2 Business Scope

Enable complete performance analytics including data aggregation, trend analysis, predictive modeling, and executive reporting. The system supports multiple analysis types, automated calculations, real-time dashboards, and audit trails while ensuring accuracy, consistency, and regulatory compliance across all performance analytics processes and organizational levels.
