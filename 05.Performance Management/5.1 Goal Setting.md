# 5.1 Goal Setting

## 5.1.1 Business Purpose

Goal Setting provides comprehensive management of employee goals, objectives, and performance targets across the organization. This ensures strategic alignment, clear performance expectations, measurable outcomes, and continuous improvement while supporting various goal frameworks, cascading objectives, and integration with performance reviews and compensation systems.

## 5.1.2 Business Scope

Enable complete goal management including goal creation, cascading, tracking, alignment, and evaluation. The system supports SMART goal framework, OKR methodology, goal alignment with business objectives, progress monitoring, and integration with performance reviews while ensuring accuracy, transparency, and strategic alignment across all organizational levels.
