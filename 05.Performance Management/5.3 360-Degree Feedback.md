# 5.3 360-Degree Feedback

## 5.3.1 Business Purpose

360-Degree Feedback provides comprehensive multi-source performance evaluation by collecting feedback from managers, peers, direct reports, and sometimes clients. This ensures well-rounded, unbiased performance assessments while supporting various feedback methodologies, anonymous feedback collection, and integration with performance reviews and development planning systems.

## 5.3.2 Business Scope

Enable complete 360-degree feedback management including feedback collection, anonymous feedback processing, feedback aggregation, and reporting. The system supports multiple feedback types, automated calculations, confidentiality controls, and audit trails while ensuring accuracy, transparency, and regulatory compliance across all feedback processes and organizational levels.
