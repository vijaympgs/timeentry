# 6.1 Training Programs

## 6.1.1 Business Purpose

Training Programs provides comprehensive management of organizational training initiatives, including program design, scheduling, delivery, and evaluation. This ensures structured, effective, and measurable training programs while supporting various training methodologies, delivery formats, and integration with performance management and career development systems.

## 6.1.2 Business Scope

Enable complete training program management including program creation, instructor assignment, resource allocation, participant enrollment, and outcome evaluation. The system supports multiple training types, automated scheduling, budget tracking, and audit trails while ensuring accuracy, compliance, and effectiveness across all training processes and organizational levels.
