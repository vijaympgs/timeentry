# 6.4 Certification Tracking

## 6.4.1 Business Purpose

Certification Tracking provides comprehensive management of professional certifications, credentials, and qualifications for employees. This ensures proper certification maintenance, compliance monitoring, and skill validation while supporting various certification types, renewal processes, and integration with performance management and learning systems.

## 6.4.2 Business Scope

Enable complete certification tracking management including certification acquisition, maintenance, renewal, and compliance monitoring. The system supports multiple certification types, automated expiration tracking, renewal notifications, and audit trails while ensuring accuracy, compliance, and effectiveness across all certification processes and organizational levels.
