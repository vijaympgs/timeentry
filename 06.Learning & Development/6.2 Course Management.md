# 6.2 Course Management

## 6.2.1 Business Purpose

Course Management provides comprehensive administration of learning content, including course creation, content organization, delivery management, and learner tracking. This ensures structured, accessible, and effective learning experiences while supporting various content types, delivery formats, and integration with training programs and competency development systems.

## 6.2.2 Business Scope

Enable complete course management including course design, content creation, instructor assignment, learner enrollment, and progress tracking. The system supports multiple course types, multimedia content, automated assessments, and audit trails while ensuring accuracy, accessibility, and effectiveness across all learning processes and organizational levels.
